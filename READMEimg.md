## Image Specifications

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Hero images:** 1920x1080px minimum (landscape, high quality)
- **Category images:** 800x800px minimum (square)
- **Product images:** 600x600px minimum (square, clear product photos)
- **About images:** 800x600px minimum (landscape)

## Image Guidelines

1. **High Quality:** Use high-resolution images that look professional
2. **Consistent Style:** Maintain a cohesive visual style across all product images
3. **Proper Lighting:** Ensure products are well-lit with clear details
4. **Background:** Use clean, simple backgrounds (white or neutral colors work best)
5. **Aspect Ratio:** Maintain the recommended aspect ratios for best display

## Where to Find Images

You can use:
- Your own product photography
- Stock photo websites (Unsplash, Pexels, etc.)
- AI image generators
- Professional photography services

## Note

The website will display a placeholder if images are missing, but for the best user experience, all images should be added before launching the site.
